Good to know

Weather & cancellations

Beach days live and die by the sky. Here's how we keep it fair when the weather doesn't cooperate.

Free reschedule or refund for weather

If rain, heavy wind, or a beach closure makes your day a no-go, reschedule for any open date or take a full refund. We'll reach out if the forecast turns before your window.

Change of plans

Cancel 24 hours ahead for a full refund. Inside 24 hours, we credit your setup toward another beach day this season. No-shows are charged in full.
